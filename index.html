Nelson Mandela

South Africa's first black head of state

Here is a timeline of Mandela's life:

  • 1918- Nelson Mandela was born
  • 1944- He joined African National Congress (ANC)
  • 1960- Many black people were arrested by the Government after protest from the ANC
  • 1962- Nelson Mandela was sent to prison
  • 1990- Nelson Mandela was released from prison
  • 1991- He became the leader of ANC
  • 1993- He won the Nobel peace price
  • 1994- He became the president of South Africa
  • 1999- He retired as the president of South Africa
  • 2013- Nelson Mandela died
